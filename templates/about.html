About Us

Since 1993

Computer Concepts began in 1979 as Professional Four, providing Word Processing, Resume Preparation, Bookkeeping/Tax Services, and Computer Programming. In 1993, we expanded to building computers, and from 2000 onward, we continued to evolve in areas such as Accounting, Tax Services, Computer Training, Software Development, and Networking. In 2005, we ventured into Digital Media production, and from 2016 to 2019, we collaborated with Liberty Tax.

We're proud to offer the highest quality, most unique merchandise on the market today. From our family to yours, we put lots of love and careful attention into each item. We hope you enjoy our work as much as we enjoy bringing it to you.
