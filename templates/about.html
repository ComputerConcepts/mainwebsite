About Computer Concept

From our family to yours, we offer innovative IT and computing services with a personal touch since 1993.

Our Journey

1979

Founded as Professional Four, offering Word Processing, Resume Preparation, Bookkeeping/Tax Services, and Computer Programming.

1993

Expanded into building computers, marking the birth of Computer Concept.

2000

Evolved to include Accounting, Tax Services, Computer Training, Software Development, and Networking.

2005

Ventured into Digital Media production, expanding our service offerings.

2016-2019

Collaborated with Liberty Tax, further enhancing our tax services.

Today

Continuing to evolve and provide cutting-edge IT solutions to meet the changing needs of our clients.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide the highest quality, most unique IT solutions on the market today. We put lots of love and careful attention into each service we offer, ensuring that our clients receive personalized and innovative solutions for their technological needs.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of comprehensive IT solutions, bridging the gap between technology and people. We strive to empower businesses and individuals with cutting-edge tools and knowledge, fostering growth and success in the digital age.
